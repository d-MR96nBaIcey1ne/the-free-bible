# Prayers to Carry

Single-tablet scripture renderings designed to be kept on a phone and returned to indefinitely. Each tablet contains a bloomed rendering in English, the source-language text (Hebrew or Greek), and a transliteration where appropriate.

A Prayer to Carry is distinct from a Prayer of the Day:

- **POD tablets** are daily-cycle — single verse, rhythm-bound, one share at a time.
- **Carry tablets** are passage-length — full bloom plus source on one tablet, carried indefinitely.

Shared chamber aesthetic across the genre: wheat-tan ground, deep-brown border, dark wood ink, Georgia typeface, single-page tablet aspect, chamber seal at close.

## Inaugural tablets

- **Romans 8:35–39 — Paul's Conviction** (Greek). The over-conquering through the One who loved us.
- **Philippians 2:5–11 — The Christ Hymn** (Greek). Christ emptied himself; therefore God over-exalted him.
- **Isaiah 53:3–5 — The Suffering Servant** (Hebrew). By his wounds we are healed.

## Format

Each tablet is provided in two formats:

- `.docx` — canonical, editable
- `.jpg` — for direct sharing on chat platforms

Both formats render identically.
